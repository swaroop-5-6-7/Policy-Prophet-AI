Security Roadmap

At Policy Prophet AI, securing your actuarial data is our top priority. Discover how we protect you today and our robust plans for the future.

Current Security Posture

Our Premium Estimator operates on principles of strict data minimization and statelessness.

  • Stateless Processing: All risk profiles are processed in real-time memory. We do not write, log, or persist your health metrics to any database.
  • Encrypted Connections: API traffic between your browser and our backend is protected by standard TLS (HTTPS) encryption.
  • Client-side Validation: Malformed or anomalous inputs are caught and rejected client-side before ever reaching our models.

Future Architectural Updates

As we scale our operations, we are taking proactive steps to transform our application into a fully compliant, enterprise-grade platform.

End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

We plan to implement zero-knowledge encryption protocols where health assertions are encrypted on your local device before transmission. Even our servers won't be able to read the raw data inputs.

Federated Learning Infrastructure

Our future models will train synchronously across decentralized user nodes. Instead of bringing data to the model, we will send the model to the data—drastically reducing exposure risks.

SOC 2 Type II & HIPAA Compliance

We are actively building our internal controls, audit trails, and data processing agreements to formally achieve certifications that demonstrate institutional compliance.

Biometric Access Control

For B2B agency users, we will introduce hardware-backed FIDO2 authentication and WebAuthn to ensure only authorized brokers can query the Prophet engines.
